What is Mindful Click?

Mindful Click is a crowd-sourced resource to track the ethics of products and companies, created by the Axiologue community. We put ethical information in the hands of consumers, so they can shop in line with their own personal ethics and support the companies that are doing it right. We are guided by the following five principles:

  • Free

    Ethical information should never be behind a paywall. We believe ethical information should be accessible to anyone who wants access to it.

  • Personalized

    Who decides what's ethical? You do! Axiologue lets users customize their ethical profiles so they only see what's important to them.

  • Open

    We believe ethical behavior starts with the free exchange of knoweldge. All of Mindful Click's raw data is freely available, via this website or our Open API. All of our software is open source.

  • Comprehensive

    Mindful Click covers all possible ethical categories, including environment, labor, political affiliation, and more.

  • Participatory

    Mindful Click is a crowd-sourced project, and all you need to contribute is a desire to bring ethical information to those who want it!

Who is Axiologue?

Axiologue is a community-based project. We are researchers, designers, teachers, artists, philosophers, programmers, students, and more. Axiologue could even be you! Learn more about how to join the Axiologue community below. We'd love to have you join our ever-growing community!

FAQ

How Does Scoring Work?

This is such an important topic that we wrote a whole blog post about it.

The short answer is that we match the facts we know about each company/product with your ethical feelings about each fact. The scores roughly translate to:

  • a postive score means the product is in line with your ethics!
  • a negative score means the product is contrary to your ethics!
  • a zero means you don't care about that issue (it could also mean we don't have any data in that particular category)!

To construct your own ethical profile, sign up for an account. If you don't want to make an account at this time, don't worry: you can browse our products and see scores for our generic profiles

 How can I help?

All our research is collected by user volunteers like you! We wrote a guide to help first-time contributors, and you should drop into our forum if you have have further questions.

 Do you only have information on shoes?

As of right now, yes. In fact, we're focusing specifically on athletic shoes. We are planning to expand to other product categories in the near future, so if you have suggestions for a certain product category you'd like us to research, please email us. We'd love to hear your suggestions!

 How do you define your ethics?

If you want to browse without making an account, Axiologue has generic profiles that represent the ethical profiles of certain stock characters (e.g. Generic Liberal, Generic Conservative). To make your own ethical profile, Sign Up and Axiologue will ask a few questions to help you set a base profile. If you want to further tweak your ethical profile, you can do so on your profile page.

 How can I request a product category?

We are currently only focused on athletic shoes, but we are planning to expand into new product categories soon. If you have an idea for what product categories you'd like to see Axiologue cover, email us or let us on on the forums.
