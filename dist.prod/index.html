FAQ

What is Axiologue?

Axiologue is a crowd-sourced, community-based project to track the ethics of products and companies. We put ethical information in the hands of consumers, so they can shop in line with their own personal ethics and support the companies that are doing it right. We are follow five principles:

  • Free: Ethical information should never be behind a paywall. We believe ethical information should be accessible to anyone who wants access to it.
  • Personalized: Ethics are deeply personal. Rather than assume some universal ethical standard, Axiologue allows you to prioritize what's important to you.
  • Comprehensive: Axiologue covers all possible ethical categories, including environment, labor, political affiliation, and more.
  • Open: All of Axiologue's data will be publically available, via our own websites/apps and an API so that other developers can access the data directly.
  • Participatory: Axiologue is a crowd-sourced project, and all you need to contribute is a desire to bring ethical information to those who want it!
Who is Axiologue?

Axiologue is a community-based projects. We are researchers, designers, teachers, artists, philosophers, programmers, students, and more. Axiologue could even be you! All of our data is collected and processed by users like you, so look at our how to contribute guide for information on how to contribute.

How Does Scoring Work?

This is such an important topic that we wrote a whole blog post about it.

The short answer is that we match the facts we know about each company/product with your ethical feelings about each fact. The scores roughly translate to:

  • a postive score means the product is in line with your ethics!
  • a negative score means the product is contrary to your ethics!
  • a zero means you don't care about that issue (it could also mean we don't have any data in that particular category)!

To construct your own ethical profile, sign up for an account. If you don't want to make an account at this time, don't worry: you can browse our products and see scores for our generic profiles

How can I help?

All our research is collected by user volunteers like you! We wrote a guide to help first-time contributors, and you should drop into our forum if you have have further questions.

Do you only have information on shoes?

As of right now, yes. In fact, we're focusing specifically on athletic shoes. We are planning to expand to other product categories in the near future, so if you have suggestions for a certain product category you'd like us to research, please email us. We'd love to hear your suggestions!
